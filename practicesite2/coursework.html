I have attended the University of Illinois as a James Scholar, Parkland College in Champaign, Illinois, Northern Virginia Community College in Manassas, Virginia, and George Mason University, where I have been on the Dean's List every semester and carry a 4.0 GPA.

At George Mason, the Bachelor of Independent Studies (BIS) degree allows students to create unique majors that utilize courses students have already taken and builds on them with new courses to create a major specific to the student.

My interest is in communications and graphic and web design. This has led me to take art and design courses, technical writing courses, and public relations courses.

I am also interested in working with nonprofit organizations and have taken courses to prepare for work in that field.
